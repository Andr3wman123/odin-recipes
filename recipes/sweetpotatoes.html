Roasted Sweet Potatoes

Delicious oven roasted sweet potatoes are sure to be a hit with your family this fall season.

Check out our recipe below!

    Ingredients
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked Paprika
    Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Wash and peel potatoes
  3. Dice into equal bite size pieces
  4. Toss with Olive Oil on a flat roasting sheet
  5. Season well with all listed spices
  6. Roast for 20 minutes, flipping potatoes halfway through
